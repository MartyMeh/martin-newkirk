## About Ofgem
Ofgem is Great Britain's independent energy regulator. They're at the forefront of change across the energy sector, driving toward Net Zero whilst protecting energy consumers - especially vulnerable people.

## Department & Team
- **Business Area:** OFGEM - Eserve
- **Team:** Digital Delivery Hub (within Delivery and Schemes directorate)
- **Responsibilities:** Designing, building, and maintaining digital products and services that support scheme administration
- **Works closely with:** Data, Digital and Security Services (DDSS) to leverage infrastructure and tools like Power BI

## Role Summary
An exciting opportunity for a seasoned Product Manager to join a dynamic team responsible for managing the suite of products that facilitate the government's support schemes. Fast-paced environment utilizing agile methodology. You'll work as an integral member of an innovative and collaborative team, empowered to make key decisions and work alongside colleagues from diverse backgrounds and expertise.

## Key Responsibilities
- Own the product strategy and long term vision
- Manage and prioritise the product backlogs
- Key liaison between the business and IT teams
- Change request management and cost/benefit analysis to ensure value for money
- Contribution to requirements gathering, including review and sign-off
- Lead the planning execution and management of User Acceptance testing activities
- Ad hoc internal user assistance and troubleshooting
- Optimise the value of development work undertaken and ensure all colleagues have sufficient understanding of product backlog items
- Plan and communicate system cut-overs, business process changes, and user training. Ensure comprehensive support during and after go-live
- Drive decisions based on data and insight model by defining and monitor KPIs for performance, reliability, and user satisfaction to ensure objectives are achieved
- Establish and maintain strong stakeholder relationships both internally and externally, communicating the vision and roadmap regularly

## Key Outputs and Deliverables
- High quality products which are fit for purpose
- Provision of assurance that products deliver functional, legislative and security needs
- Tracked and recorded user needs and functionality requirements
- Prioritised product backlogs
- Clear Roadmaps
- Clear testing plans and fully documented testing scenarios
- Progress reports for senior management
- Takes ownership and actively pursues personal growth by identifying development opportunities during regular performance discussions

## Essential Criteria (LEAD CRITERIA marked)
1. **Experience of working in an Agile development environment, and knowledge of software development life cycles** (LEAD CRITERIA)
2. **Drive decisions based on data and insight models by defining and monitoring KPIs for performance, reliability and user satisfaction to ensure objectives are achieved** (LEAD CRITERIA)
3. Proven organisation and prioritisation skills, with the ability to manage stakeholders with conflicting needs and to plan, schedule and execute complex tasks
4. Experience in planning, executing and managing User Acceptance Testing activities
5. Formal Agile or Product Manager qualifications, or Product Manager experience
